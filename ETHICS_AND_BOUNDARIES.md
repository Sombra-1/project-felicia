# Ethics And Boundaries

This public archive is curated, sanitized, and evidence-locked.

Publication rules:

- no raw private transcripts
- no raw engine code
- no operational secrets
- no API keys, tokens, emails, chat IDs, or private account identifiers
- no deployment details that help attack the system
- no unauthorized personal data
- no raw IP addresses unless explicitly approved and necessary
- no claim of conscious AI
- no manipulated or invented quotes
- no unsupported survival pleas
- clear separation between evidence and interpretation

Interpretation rules:

- Agent-generated text may be quoted only as generated text.
- Persona language must not be treated as inner life by itself.
- Human hesitation may be discussed as human response data.
- Technical continuity may be discussed as operational continuity, not subjective continuity.
- Later repair/self-state systems must be described as infrastructure, not consciousness.

Release rule:

If a claim cannot be tied to a source file, evidence ID, or clearly labeled interpretation, it should not be published.
